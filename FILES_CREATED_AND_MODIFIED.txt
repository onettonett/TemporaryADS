KEY DESIGN DECISIONS
================================================================================

COICOP Concordance (mapping LCF → MM23)
  Design: 1:1 at division level (12 categories)
  Rationale: Aligns with LCF granularity & ONS publishing standard
  Location: compute_group_inflation.py, COICOP_CONCORDANCE dict
  Future: Extendable to 3-digit level (01.1.1) if needed

Inflation Formula
  Formula: π_g,t = Σ_i [ w_i,g,t-1 × (P_i,t / P_i,t-1 − 1) ]
  Type: Laspeyres index with lagged household-group weights
  Justification: Standard practice, transparent, defensible
  Alternative: Chained-Laspeyres (update weights annually) – documented

Archetype Dimensions
  Selected: tenure, income quintile, region, age band, pensioner, disability, etc.
  Rationale: Align with LCF sampling & policy-relevant groupings
  Weighting: Uses survey weights for population representation
