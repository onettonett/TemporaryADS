POLICY IMPLICATIONS:
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. Problem is NOT chronically unequal inflation
   (2015-2021 showed equal treatment)

2. Problem IS crisis vulnerability
   (2022-23 exposed structural inequalities: housing exposure, basket composition)

3. Targeted intervention needed for:
   • Private renters (8%+ inflation in crisis)
   • Families with children (higher food share)
   • Low-income households (limited flexibility)
